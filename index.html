Why Rihanna?

Why is Rihanna one of the most prominent artist's of the 21st-century?

It’s not uncommon for artists in the music industry to be multi-talented, but it's pretty rare for artists to be very successful in business, the fashion industry and their music career. Rihanna is a global pop and fashion icon, who continues to push boundaries and records as a singer, songwriter, businesswoman, performer, and actress.

The bad-gal Riri is well-known for her powerful vocal range and diverse music style but has become a true fashion icon due to her profound fashion style and creativity at the MET Gala. She is also the second best-selling female music artist of all time and the highest certified female artist of all time on the RIAA’s digital ranking. She didn’t just stop there, she created and launched two flourishing businesses. The first one being “Fenty Beauty” and the second being “Savage X Fenty”. These companies are well known for breaking beauty standards and their extensive range of inclusivity.

From selling over 250 million records worldwide, to being the most watched performer at the super bowl half-time, then being the wealthiest female musician, and lastly being a humanitarian. You can say for certain that Riri has put in “work work work”.
